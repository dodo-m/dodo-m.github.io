相似性定理：f(x)⊃F(s)⇒f(as)⊃1∣a∣F(sa)f(x)\supset F(s)\Rightarrow f(as)\supset\frac{1}{|a|}F(\frac{s}{a})f(x)⊃F(s)⇒f(as)⊃∣a∣1​F(as​).

加性定理：f(x)⊃F(s),g(x)⊃G(s)⇒f(x)+g(x)⊃F(s)+G(s)f(x)\supset F(s),g(x)\supset G(s)\Rightarrow f(x)+g(x)\supset F(s)+G(s)f(x)⊃F(s),g(x)⊃G(s)⇒f(x)+g(x)⊃F(s)+G(s).

移位定理：f(x)⊃F(s)⇒f(x−a)⊃e−i2πasF(s).f(x)\supset F(s)\Rightarrow f(x-a)\supset e^{-i2\pi as}F(s).f(x)⊃F(s)⇒f(x−a)⊃e−i2πasF(s).

调制定理：f(x)⊃F(s)⇒f(x)cos(ωx)⊃12F(s−ω/2π)+12F(s+ω/2π)f(x)\supset F(s)\Rightarrow f(x)cos(\omega x)\supset \frac{1}{2}F(s-\omega/2\pi)+\frac{1}{2}F(s+\omega/2\pi)f(x)⊃F(s)⇒f(x)cos(ωx)⊃21​F(s−ω/2π)+21​F(s+ω/2π).

卷积定理：f(x)⊃F(s),g(x)⊃G(s)⇒f(x)∗g(x)⊃F(s)G(s)f(x)\supset F(s),g(x)\supset G(s)\Rightarrow f(x)*g(x)\supset F(s)G(s)f(x)⊃F(s),g(x)⊃G(s)⇒f(x)∗g(x)⊃F(s)G(s).

卷积满足交换律、结合律和分配律：

  • f∗g=g∗ff*g=g*ff∗g=g∗f（交换律）
  • f∗(g∗h)=(f∗g)∗hf*(g*h)=(f*g)*hf∗(g∗h)=(f∗g)∗h（结合律）
  • f∗(g∗h)=f∗g+f∗hf*(g*h)=f*g+f*hf∗(g∗h)=f∗g+f∗h（分配律）

  • f∗(gh)‾=f‾(g‾∗h‾)\overline{f*(gh)}=\overline{f}(\overline{g}*\overline{h})f∗(gh)​=f​(g​∗h)

瑞利定理：∫−∞∞∣f(x)∣2dx=∫−∞∞∣F(s)∣2ds\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|f(x)|^2dx=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|F(s)|^2ds∫−∞∞​∣f(x)∣2dx=∫−∞∞​∣F(s)∣2ds.

功率定理：∫−∞∞f(x)g∗(x)dx=∫−∞∞F(s)G∗(s)ds\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x)g^*(x)dx=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}F(s)G^*(s)ds∫−∞∞​f(x)g∗(x)dx=∫−∞∞​F(s)G∗(s)ds.
